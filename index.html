Impact of Trump's Social Media Posts on Prediction Markets

This project investigates the impact of Donald Trump's social media posts on prediction markets. We analyze publicly available social media data together with historical polymarket data to explore potential relationships between online statements and changes in prediction market prices.

Ethics

For ethics we needed to considet two things:
  • We are scraping and using the posts from Donald Trump. Since Donald Trump is a public figure and his posts on truthsocial are considered as an official communication channel of the US administracion. Therfore we dont thing using the posts causes any ethical concern.
  • The Polymarket dataset we are using contains userdata, in the form of usernames and ids. In our Analysis we dont consider userdata at all and only focus on marked data. With just the given information in the dataset, it is not possible to indentify an individual user.

Data Collection and Wrangling

We downloaded our Polymarket data from the following publicly available dataset: Polymarket dataset on Hugging Face .

Example entries from the markets.parquet dataset:

For the post history of Donald Trump we downloaded an already existing Dataset from "https://datadrivendecisionlab.com/resources?resource=trump-tweets-dataset", which provided posts from 2009 to the end of 2025. To get more recent posts from truth social, we wrote a simple scraper. The scraper was written in python using selenium. The final data was scraped on the 10th of june and we scraped post gong back to the 25 of December, to complete missing data. Two weeks after the last time the scraper was used, the RUB network was fingerprinted by cloudflare and we couldnt gather more recent posts.

to make it possible to work with the data we removed some of the Posts:

  • Posts only containing images or videos where removed, since implementing immage recognition for topic recognition was out of scope.
  • we removed Post which did only retweet a diffrent post without adding more text, since those are also implementet as immages.
We also needed to convert timestamps into a consistant format and timezone. Since the Polymarket timestamps are Unittime, we diecided to convert the timestamps of the Dataset and the scraped data to utc so they con easily converted to unittime.
