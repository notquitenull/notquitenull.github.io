Results

Meta Results

Trumps social media posts have no correlation with the majority of markets which remain after the pruning step. Out of the 20074 markets in the analysis output ~41.13% have at least one post with significant correlation. For of the posts which were matched to a market slightly above half were significant to some market.

Grouping the markets by topic and performing the same analysis shows that there seems to be no direct correlation to number of markets in a category to the percentage of markets with (at least one) statistically significant social media post.
