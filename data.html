Donald Trump post history

For the post history of Donald Trump we downloaded an already existing Dataset from https://datadrivendecisionlab.com/resources?resource=trump-tweets-dataset , which provided posts from 2009 to the end of 2025. To get more recent posts from Truth Social, we wrote a simple scraper. The scraper was written in python using selenium. The final data was scraped on the 10th of june and we scraped post gong back to the 25 of December, to complete missing data. Two weeks after the last time the scraper was used, the RUB network was fingerprinted by cloudflare and we couldnt gather more recent posts.

The Scraper we build use Seleneium (a browser automation and testing tool) and Beautiful Soup (an HTML parsing library), to automatiklie visit Twitter and Trouth Social. We needed boath Selenium and Beautiful Soup even though boath should have been enough. Selenium was used in the non headless mode to further prevent fingerprinting and prevention by the cloudflare bot protection and Beautiful Soup was used for parsing, because we had some problems, while searching for specific HTML DOM elements, while using Seleniums methods. The Scraper for twitter goest through the already downloaded daterset and completes tweets which may be cut short, baucaus of the old character limti, by visiting the twwed directly, via its id. The Trouth Social scraper starts on Donalstrumps (ore any other profiel) post history and goes through it, beginning with the newest post. While doing so it filters out advertised posts and seaves relevant information in an seperate CSV File, which needed to be merged with the already existing dataset.

Scraping content from Twitter and Truth Social breaks the TOS of both platforms. We decided to do it anyways, since the alternative would have been to pay Elon Musk (Twitter) and Donald Trump (Truth Social) money, to use their respective APIs.

Data Processing

What we did wiht the collected data:
  1. converting the diffrent timestamps into one timezone
  2. Filter out posts with only videos or images or pure retweets
  3. merging the scraped posts wiht the downloaded and commpletet dataset
  4. Assign topics to each market and post using ML
  5. Enter into shared database for correlation analysis

The first three steps where performed in one script. We descided to convert the already existing timestamps into gmt (utc). The dowloaded and updated dataset was using utc+1 and Trouth Social data had utc-4 or utc-5 depending on the season. For the conversion we used the Pendulum Python library, to reduce mistakes while parsing and conversion.
We then filtert out posts, which only contained images or videos, which also only contain the String "[Immage]" or "[Video]" inside the CSV Post text field. We chose to exclude those, since topic labeling immages and videos is out of scope for uns, but could be added in the future.
We also decided to exclude retweets, which are just the retweets without added text by the poster, we internaly called those pure retweets. Those where easy to exclude, since they where either jsut immages, which are already filtered out or theire text fiels contained only re: {link to the other tweeet}, for which we used a simple regular expression.
After the filtering and conversion was finished the two CSV files where joint together. The resulting CSV was stored in the GITHub reposetory.
The CSV contains around 85700 Posts, but only around 35000 are usefull for us since the majority of Trumps posts where postet befor Polymarket was founded in june of 2020
